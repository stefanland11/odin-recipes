Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Recipe

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is one of the more difficult meals to prepare. Here is a step by step guide to help you through this arduous task.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of spread
  3. Combine both slices of bread into a sandwich
