Pizza Recipe

Who doesn't love a good pizza recipe? Learn how to make a delicious pizza right at home.

Ingredients

Dough

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar

Toppings

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients for dough in a bowl
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Cover and let sit for 20 minutes
  4. Form the dough into a circle
  5. Add tomato sauce
  6. Add cheese
  7. Add desired toppings
  8. Bake in the oven at 400 degrees for twenty minutes
